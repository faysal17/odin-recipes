Fresh from the White Sea Sky Fish Saute

Fried horse mackerel with melted cheese

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 whole horse mackerel
  • 1 ball of mozzarella cheese
  • salt to taste
  • white pepper to taste
  • 4 tsp. flour
  • 1 egg
  • 6 tbsp. panko
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 onion
  • 6 tbsp. ketchup
  • 2 tsp. vinegar
  • 2 tsp. olive oil
  • vegetable oil
  • hot sauce to taste
  • red onion
  • basil

STEPS

  1. Split the horse mackerel for frying, leaving a hinge. Cut the mozzarella into halves and then into one-third inch slices.

  2. Mince the tomato and onion for the sauce and mix with hot sauce.

  3. Sprinkle salt and white pepper on mackerel, then place mozzarella slices in between the fillets. Dip into flour, beaten egg, and panko in order.

  4. Fill frying pan with about one-third inch of vegetable oil and heat to 320F. Add breaded mackerel. Fry about five minutes total, turning over once.
    Remove and place on a cooling rack over a paper towel-lined tray to drain oil.

  5. Slice red onion thinly and garnish fish with sauce mixture and basil.
